Other ways you can control tracking

To show you better ads, we use data that advertisers and other partners provide us about your activity off Meta Company Products, including websites and apps. You can control whether we use this data to show you ads in your ad settings.

The Facebook Audience Network is a way for advertisers to show you ads in apps and websites off the Meta Company Products. One of the ways Audience Network shows relevant ads is by using your ad preferences to determine which ads you may be interested in seeing.
