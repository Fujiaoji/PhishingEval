Road Running Shoes

Road running shoes are some of the most popular trainers on the market. That's because they move seamlessly from sidewalk to road to paved indoor track, and you can also use them on the treadmill. But finding the road running shoes that work for you takes a bit of self-discovery.
Trail Running Shoes

When you're hitting the trail for a run, you want shoes on your feet you can trust to protect you the entire way. Trail running shoes are designed to keep you stable and sure-footed as you traverse terrain that may throw rocks, roots and even small streams your way.
Walking Shoes

Whether you're building up to 10,000 steps a day, moving through a Couch to 5k program or you're already an avid runner, there's something for you in the Brooks line of walking and running shoes. Shop walking shoes that offer stabilization and comfort as well as road running shoes with plenty of cushion to keep you bouncing along.
Track & Cross Country Shoes

Level up your competitive running performance with a pair of track shoes or cross country spikes. Browse the selection of competitive running and training shoes to find the type that works best where you like to run.
