Stathead.com

Are you a Stathead? Then you've come to the right place. Stathead subscribers gain access to a suite of tools used by the pros. Search the best (or the worst) seasons, games, splits and more by players and teams throughout history. Any time you see an incredible stat mentioned on a broadcast, in an article or in social media, there's an excellent chance it came from Stathead. Every subscription also comes with ad-free browsing on our site for that sport so you get the smoothest browsing experience. Stathead Baseball, Stathead Football, Stathead Hockey and Stathead Basketball are live now, with more sports planned for the future. You can also sign up for our free newsletters to stay up on the latest scores, stats and news in your favorite sports.

Baseball-Reference.com

The premier online database for the history of Major League Baseball. Pages for every season, team, player, league and manager in major league history. Every AL, NL and Federal League box score since 1901. Every line of play-by-play since 1973. Also includes sections for minor, foreign, independent and college baseball leagues. Updated daily.

Basketball-Reference.com

The leading online encyclopedia for NBA history. Pages for every season, team, player, coach and draft in NBA/BAA/ABA history. Every box score in NBA/BAA/ABA history. Every line of play-by-play since 1996-97. Every buzzer-beater in NBA/BAA/ABA history. Also includes sections for G-League, WNBA, NBL, Olympics and foreign professional leagues. Updated daily.

Pro-Football-Reference.com

The preeminent website detailing the full statistical history of the NFL (including the APFA, AAFC and AFL). Pages for every season, team, player, coach and draft. Line scores for every game detailing every score in history, box scores since 1960 and play-by-play since 1994. Updated the morning after every NFL game.

Hockey-Reference.com

The best online database for NHL statistical history. Pages for every season, team, player, coach and draft in NHL history. Every box score in NHL history. Also includes coverage of the WHA. Updated daily.

College Football @ Sports-Reference.com

College Football game results for every major school back to Princeton vs Rutgers in 1869. FBS player Stats back to 1956. Every FBS box score since 2000. The site also includes sections for coaches, awards, leaders, and bowl games. Use the Play Index to create your own custom searches.

College Basketball @ Sports-Reference.com

College Basketball Stats and Game Results. Game results for every game featuring two major schools since 1949. Player Stats from Stephen Curry to Bill Walton. Stats for schools and conferences. The site also includes sections for coaches, awards, leaders, and the postseason. Every box score in NCAA Tournament history and every regular season box score since 2010. Use the Play Index to create your own custom searches.

FBref.com

Comprehensive website for football/soccer statistics from all over the world. Statistics, scores and history for over 100,000 players from over 100 competitions, including men's, women's, club and national teams. Expected Goals and other advanced stats from StatsBomb for a dozen competitions dating back to 2017. Updated daily.
