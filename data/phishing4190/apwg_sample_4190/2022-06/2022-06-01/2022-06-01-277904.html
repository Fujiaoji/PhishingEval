Frequently asked questions

What is DHL?

DHL International GmbH is a German international courier, package delivery and express mail service.

What kind of support does DHL offer?

Customer service team of DHL offers the following support channels: email, live chat, call center, social media support and self-service support.

 

How do I contact DHL?

You can contact DHL by emailing them, starting a chat with a live agent on their website, calling their hotline or by leaving them a message on one of the social media platforms. Alternatively, you can use their knowledge base if you prefer self-service support.

 

What is DHL's email address?

You can contact their support by writing an email to mnl.customercare@dhl.com or by filling out the form.

Does the DHL have live chat?

Yes, DHL has live chat support. Go to the site: https://www.dhl.com/global-en/home/contact-us.html

How do I speak to someone in DHL?

If you prefer call support, you can contact their helpline +1800 111 345
