Aruba and data protection

 

Your data is
in Europe

  

Your data is in Europe

Aruba S.p.A. is an Italian company based in Italy: your personal and administrative data is managed in Europe, in accordance with European laws. Aruba uses it exclusively to provide the services you have purchased. Your data is not sold to third parties, because our business is not to commercialize our customers' data.

Data Centers in Italy and
in Europe, guaranteed

  

Data Centers in Italy and in Europe, guaranteed

Our main services, such as email, hosting and PEC (certified email) are managed exclusively within our Italian data centers. These are infrastructures that we own, which are secure, reliable and compliant with the highest standards (Rating 4) ANSI/TIA 942. Only Cloud services can be hosted in our European network of data centers, as well as in Italy. But you and you alone will be able to choose where your Cloud infrastructure is based.

You and you alone
control your data

  

You and you alone control your data

The data you store with Aruba is completely under your control. We guarantee that we will not access nor use the data that you put in our infrastructure. We will not access the data in your emails, hosting or Cloud. We won't sell your data to third parties and we won't commercialize your data. As well as this, in the Cloud, everything is certified too, as our services comply with the CISPE Code of Conduct.

The highest standards
when it comes to privacy and confidentiality

  

The highest standards when it comes to privacy and confidentiality

As we are an Italian company and our data centers are based in Europe, our infrastructure is not subject to US laws and regulations such as the Cloud Act or the FISA (Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act), which actually allow unlimited access to data and information, with a significant impact on privacy.

ISO 27001
certification

  

ISO 27001 certification

Aruba has been certified for many years, and complies with the logical, physical and organizational standards imposed by ISO 27001 certification. We also have many other certificates, including ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and ANSI/TIA 942. See our certificates.

Infrastructure
security

  

Infrastructure security

Aruba's data centers represent true excellence, thanks to the latest technology, certified security standards and total respect for the environment. This means that we can guarantee the logical and physical security and redundancy of all our systems. Visit the security section of the data centers.

Green-by-design

Your IT infrastructure with zero environmental impact

Aruba digital services, together with cloud and data center services chosen by our customers, are completely sustainable and with zero environmental impact, thanks to our energy production and our procurement of additional energy requirements exclusively from GO green sources.

Eco-sustainability and certificates

Self-produced energy

We use our own hydroelectric plants and photovoltaic systems installed on the walls and roofs of our facilities, to produce clean energy that power our data centers.

Guarantee of Origin (GO)

To cover energy requirements above what we are able to produce ourselves, we buy 100% Green Energy, electricity with guarantee of origin, produced exclusively from renewable sources.
