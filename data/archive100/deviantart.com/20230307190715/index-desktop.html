Boost a deviation with the Hype badge! by team, journal

Boost a deviation with the Hype badge!

Feb 9, 2023
In response to community requests, the Hype badge, which boosts deviations to a larger audience on Browse, is now a permanent feature on DeviantArt! More people seeing art often leads to more comments and favorites, which can encourage an artist and really brighten their day. How the Hype Badge Works Deviations that receive a Hype badge will be boosted to more peoples Home pages for two days! Hype badges can be granted for 100 fragments, and the deviant who receives it will also get 10 fragments. You can only award a Hype badge to a deviation once. While posts and comments can be given a Hype badge, they won't receive any boosting effect. The Hype badge is replacing the Emerald badge, which cost the same amount of fragments to grant. Start boosting deviations! Need fragments to get your Hype on? Upgrade to Core to get fragments each week!

Add banners to promote your Premium Galleries!

Feb 15, 2023
You can now promote your Premium Galleries with banners on your deviations and Profile! Premium Galleries are a simple way for artists to sell multiple exclusive artworks at once through a one-time purchase. For deviants that currently offer Premium Galleries for sale or want to in the future, you can highlight your Premium Galleries with special banners that will display on your deviations and Profile. These banners are a useful tool to promote your works for sale and give you more opportunity to be seen by potential buyers! Banner Locations Similar to banners for Subscriptions and Commissions, you can use this feature to promote Premium Galleries in four distinct places. Two banners can be added on your deviations, one beneath your artwork and one in the sidebar. On your Profile, a banner can be included above your cover art.
