Nightfall

Humans submerge in each other's comfort and warmth, Blood buzzes with excitement when they feel each other close by. They suffocate by emotions clouding their face. Souls, that know that they were missing a piece. Both souls met each other when they were very little, yet were dubious and unaware. Simply two, who had met within the void of fate, answered with the clarity of an epiphany. You envisioned a soul falling from the firmament, not expecting someone to catch them as they were bound to fall, You assumed that you were destined to fall, Yet, there is this sea inclined to catch you. I am all disposed to unlearn how to swim, just so I could drown in the billows of your comfort. Your waters are as pellucid as crystals, I can see a reflection of myself in you.

Seeing Through Her Eyes

I still remember the little girl who I used to be So many years ago. The girl who made wishes on stars, Who saw fairies in the forest, Who looked for the pot of gold at the end of the rainbow, Who would always look for the secret passage She just knew had to be somewhere in her house. The girl who wouldn't think twice about Splashing in the biggest puddles she could find, Or riding her bike at top speed down the hill, Screaming her defiance into the wind. The girl who believed in true love more than anything, Who believed that all her dreams would come true, Who believed that anything was possible. She still exists, that little girl, Somewhere deep inside of me. Some days I wish I could be her again, Because I miss the days when everything felt Like an adventure, When everything was full of magic and wonder. Then I realized that the world can still look that way If I look again at life Through the eyes of that little girl. Life is still full of magic and adventure. I just have to learn
