Here's a little quality-of-life improvement tip to start your Spider-man game faster:

1. Go to your steam library.
2. Right click 'Marvel's Spider-man Remastered'.
3. Click 'Properties'.
4. On the first tab 'GENERAL', scroll to the bottom called 'LAUNCH OPTIONS'.
5. In the text box, type in "-nolauncher" without the " " quote.
6. Close the Properties box. The next time you launch your game, it will skip the launch menu and start the game right away.

Cheers!
